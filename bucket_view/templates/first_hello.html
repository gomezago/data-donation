What Happens When You Donate?

When you contribute to a project by donating your data you consent to your data being accessible to Data Receivers for the duration of the project. If at any point you want to delete your data or revoke access to your donation(s) you can do this directly from the DDDD Platform. In this case your data will be deleted or Data Receivers will no longer have access to it.

Create your Own Project

If you want to start a project that could benefit from collaborations impulsed by data and you think Designerly Data Donation can help you meet your goals feel free to contact us by sending an email to: datadonation-ide@tudelft.nl.
