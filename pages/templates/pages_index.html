Data about you. With you.

As we navigate the physical and digital world we leave behind an immense trail of data about us, describing what we do and how we do it. This trail of data is highly valuable for designers and researchers everywhere. However, it is often limited as it lacks details that only we can provide.
Through Designerly Data Donation we invite you to contribute to design and research projects addressing complex societal challenges by donating and enriching your personal data.

What is Designerly Data Donation?

Designerly Data Donation is intended to act as a bridge between designers and researchers (experts in their field), and people (experts in their experiences).
Designers and researchers, working on projects addressing complex societal challenges, become data receivers inviting you to contribute with your data and unique expertise. While you become a data donor, by directing your data towards projects and providing valuable complementary insights.
