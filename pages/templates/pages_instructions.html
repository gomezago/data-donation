How to Get a Copy of your Data

{% if project.id == 'ddd_voxpop'%}

Information on how to obtain a copy of your data for the {{ project.title }} project:

  1. Go to takeout.google.com and log in with your Google credentials if necessary.
    Go to > Select data to include and click on > Deselect all
  2. Scroll down until you find > My Activity and select it by ticking the box
  3. Click on > Multiple formats this should open a pop-up where you can choose specific formats for your download.
    Under > Activity records click on JSON.
  4. Click on > All activity data included, this should open a pop-up where you can choose specific activity data to export.
    Click on > Deselect all and tick only the box next to Assistant
  5. Under > My Activity you should see JSON and 2 other formats and 1 product selected
  6. Scroll down and click on the > Next step button.
    Under > Choose file type, frequency & destination select > Export once, > .zip, and > 2 GB. Click on the Create export button
  7. You will receive an email when your data is ready. Download the .zip file and upload it in the {{ project.title }} page
{% elif project.id == 'ddd_sports' %}
Garmin Users
  1. Go to https://www.garmin.com/account/datamanagement/ and log in with your Garmin Connect Credentials.
  2. Click on Export your Data
  3. Click on Request Data Export
  4. You will receive an email when your data is ready. Download the .zip file and upload it in the {{ project.title }} page
Apple Watch Users
  1. On your iPhone go to your Apple Health App and click on the icon with your profile in the upper right corner
  2. Scroll down and click on Export All Health Data
  3. Confirm by clicking on Export
  4. Upload the .zip file in the {{ project.title }} page
