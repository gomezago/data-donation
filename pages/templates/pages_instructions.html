How to Get a Copy of your Data

{% if project.id == 'ddd_voxpop'%}

Information on how to obtain a copy of your data for the {{ project.title }} project:

  1. Go to takeout.google.com and log in with your Google credentials if necessary
  2. Under Select data to include click on Deselect all
  3. Scroll down until you find My Activity and select it by ticking the box
  4. Click on All activity data included, this should open a pop-up where you can choose specific activity data to export. Click on Deselect all and tick only the box next to Assistant
  5. Click on Multiple formats, this should open a pop-up where you can choose specific formats for your download. Under Activity records click on JSON
  6. Under My Activity you should see JSON and 2 other formats and 1 product selected
  7. Scroll down and click on the Next step button
  8. Under Choose file type, frequency & destination select Export once, .zip, and 2 GB
  9. Click on the Create export button. You will receive an email when your data is ready
  10. Once your data is ready, download the .zip file and upload it in the {{ project.title }} page
