Who are we?

This project is built and maintained by a group of people within the Data-Centric Design Lab. We are part of the Knowledge and Intelligence Design (KInD) section within the faculty of Industrial Design Engineering.
We see data donation as a way of supporting responsible data collection practices for data-centric design projects. If you want to collaborate with us, or you have a project that could gain value from data donation do not hesitate to contact us.
